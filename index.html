About Us

We are 12th grade students(2021-22), we have created a collection of some of our favourite games that we played and enjoyed as children. Today we got an incredible chance to create them ourselves and face the developing challenges as junior developers.

It was quite challenging as some things did not quite work out as we wanted but after much deliberation we reached where we wanted the games to be. It's been an exhilarating ride. Just as enjoyable as playing the games themselves.
